## Overview

UBC Studios is an award-winning media production studio at the University of British Columbia. We collaborate with UBC experts to translate ideas into educational, innovative, and inspirational media-rich content.

We create quality media for education, research and brand communications. UBC Studios offers services for every budget including Do-It-Yourself media, teaching and learning support, and full professional media production services.
