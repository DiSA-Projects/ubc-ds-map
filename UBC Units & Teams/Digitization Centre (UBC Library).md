## Overview

The UBC Library Digitization Centre embraces new technologies, methods of access, workflows and preservation strategies while building sustainable digital collections to support and enrich the educational, cultural and economic endeavors of the University, the people of British Columbia and communities beyond.

## Services

* [Digitization](https://digitize.library.ubc.ca/work/) (work in collaboration with UBC departments, library branches and other groups within UBC, create digital resources available through Open Collections)
* [Digital Preservation](https://digitize.library.ubc.ca/work/)
	* [[Archivematica]] (persistent digital collections) [(website)](https://www.archivematica.org/en/)
	* LOCKSS (international community initiative and open-source technology that helps institutions preserve their own copies of digital content)
	* Member of [COPPUL (Council of Prairie and Pacific Libraries)](https://coppul.ca/)
* [Web Archiving](https://digitize.library.ubc.ca/work/) (archiving freely available web content for preservation and access since 2013, goal to preserve and make accessible web content that contributes to University's research, teaching, and learning)
	* [Archive-It](https://archive-it.org/) (web archiving service from the Internet Archive) - [[UBC Archive-It Collections]] [(website)](https://archive-it.org/organizations/734)
