## Overview

EML is an experimental space where faculty, students, and staff from all disciplines collaborate with industry and the community. Its mission is to evolve learning by creating tools and techniques using emerging media.

## EMLx

EMLx is a student-led group within EML that aims to explore and experiment with different technologies. Any student who is interested in emerging technologies and looking for a space to be creative with emerging hardware and software is welcome!

Are you a student and interested in joining EMLx, use [this form](https://ubc.ca1.qualtrics.com/jfe/form/SV_43mYa32eLXW8B4a) to let them know.
