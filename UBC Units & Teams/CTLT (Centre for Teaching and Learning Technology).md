## Overview

The Centre for Teaching, Learning and Technology (CTLT) supports teaching and learning at UBC Vancouver through professional development, the integration of learning technology, and the development and delivery of courses and programs (including face-to-face, blended/hybrid, and online modes of delivery).

CTLT offers many services and supports for the UBC Vancouver teaching and learning community, including: teaching practice development, academic program design and renewal, course design, Indigenous engagement, inclusion and accessibility, support for teaching and learning funding programs, and educational evaluation and research. The CTLT is also home to the Institute for the Scholarship of Teaching and Learning (ISoTL) and manages the [[Teaching and Learning Enhancement Fund (TLEF)]].

### Services

The CTLT offers leadership and support in teaching and learning scholarship, course and program development, teaching practice development, integration of learning technology into teaching and learning practices, and more.

* Academic program design and renewal
* Educational evaluation and research
* Funding opportunities ([[Teaching and Learning Enhancement Fund (TLEF)]])
* Inclusion and accessibility
* Indigenous engagement
* [Learning technology](https://ctlt.ubc.ca/learning-technology/) (see also [[LTIC (Learning Technology Innovation Centre)]])
* Teaching and learning professional development
