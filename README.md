### Workshops

#### Learning Technology Hub
* **Virtual Drop-Ins**
	* [LT Hub Drop-Ins](https://lthub.ubc.ca/support/training-and-professional-development/) (Mon-Fri, 9:00am-4:30pm), one-one-help with pedagogy strategies, tool training, and troubleshooting technical issues
	* [Qualtrics Studio](https://lthub.ubc.ca/support/training-and-professional-development/) (Variable days once a month), training on Qualtrics for surveys and research analysis of survey data
	* [WordPress and Wiki Clinics](https://lthub.ubc.ca/support/training-and-professional-development/) (Thu 1:00pm-3:00pm), one-on-one support for WordPress and Wiki for teaching, learning, or administrative purposes. Specialists available.
* **Self-Paced Course**
	* [Canvas 101](https://lthub.ubc.ca/support/training-and-professional-development/) (Any day/time), self-paced course designed to provide overview of features and functionality. Highlights ways to leverage Canvas to design, deliver, and manage your course
	* [Introduction to Teaching Online](https://lthub.ubc.ca/support/training-and-professional-development/) (Any day/time), learn to adapt your course to the online environment and prepare to teach online. 
* **Workshop**
	* [Generative AI Studios](https://lthub.ubc.ca/support/training-and-professional-development/) (Wed 1:00pm-2:00pm), 60-minute weekly sessions for faculty and staff considering the use of Generative AI tools as part of their teaching, researching, or daily work. Facilitated by UBC technologists and pedagogy experts. Insights into what's new, tool-of-the-week demonstration, and open Q&A
	* [Getting Start with Canvas/Kaltura/Zoom](https://lthub.ubc.ca/support/training-and-professional-development/) (Periodically held), technical and pedagogical workshops for Canvas, Kaltura, and Zoom. Workshops can be requested and scheduled.
