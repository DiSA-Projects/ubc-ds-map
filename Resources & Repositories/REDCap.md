## Overview

The REDCap platform is a secure web application for building and managing research data collection instruments. The platform is specifically designed to support online or offline data capture for research studies.

The REDCap platform runs on server infrastructure physically located in British Columbia, Canada, at the UBC University Data Centre (UDC).
