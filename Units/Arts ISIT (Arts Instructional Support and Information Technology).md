## Overview

**Arts Instructional Support & Information Technology (Arts ISIT)** at UBC Vancouver is your dedicated technology support unit and first point of contact for providing **instructional** and **technical solutions** within the Faculty of Arts. We support all Arts faculty and staff with their teaching, learning, and administrative needs and provide consultation, set up, training, and troubleshooting services for a wide range of technologies.
